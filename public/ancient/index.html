The Pyramid of the Llama Oracle

In the heart of an uncharted jungle lies the Pyramid of the Llama Oracle, a sacred monument built by an ancient civilization known as the Codexians. Within its labyrinthine chambers, the Codexians encoded their wisdom into mystical artifacts—tools that could answer questions, chart journeys, and solve riddles. At the core of the pyramid, the Llama Oracle harmonizes these artifacts, summoning their powers to aid explorers. Your quest is to uncover the secrets of this ancient system and master its orchestration.
