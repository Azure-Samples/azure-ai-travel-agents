The Enchanted Kingdom of Azure AI Agents

In the mystical Kingdom of Azure, the Grand Mage LlamaIndex wields the power of orchestrating enchanted agents to fulfill the kingdom's travel needs. These agents, each a master of their own craft, work together to create magical itineraries, recommend wondrous destinations, and uncover secrets from the vast web of knowledge. Bound by the ancient Model Context Protocol (MCP), these agents communicate seamlessly, their spells harmonized by the Triage Mage. It is your destiny, brave hero, to explore this enchanted realm and uncover the secrets of its magical codebase.
